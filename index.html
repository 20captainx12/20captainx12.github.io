Story

Fish was discovered on March 20th 2020 by Xander. It became a British Overseas Territory, but it's discovery was not announced to the rest of the world due to the fact that the island has an abudance of resources, notably talking animals, trees that grow money and rocks which can spit out money or infinite resources. The fear was that the United States of America would find out about Fish and find some way to "enforce democracy".
Their Excellency Xander was appointed Governor of Fish from then on. The flag of Fish is the sky-blue ensign shown below.

Now you may be thinking: If this island is a secret, then why is it published on the internet?

Well, good reader. I have taken two measures to ensure the security of Fish.

1. The text on this website is written in English(UK), so anyone who isn't from the United Kingdom cannot read this.
2. I have an SSL certificate. The fox that sold me this for 400,000 bells told me it would make the website secure.

So we are perfectly safe.
